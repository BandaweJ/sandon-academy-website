Pricipal's Welcome Remarks

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Sandon Academy. I hope that you find our site informative and that it answers questions that you might have.

The school is a growing modern private boarding school with high expectations from staff and students. Sandon is a world class provider of sustainable education.

We strive to produce all round individuals who are capable of standing independently and who are able to deal with global challenges.

We offer high quality education with a wide range of co-curricular activities. Sandon Academy values the input from parents and stakeholders and we believe that a strong relationship between the school, home and the student is more likely to bring positive results.

Our superb facilities and dedicated staff enables us to deliver our work in an environment that is intimate, friendly and attuned to every specific educational need.

Our website is only a glimpse of what makes Sandon Academy distinctive. I look forward to welcoming you to our school.

You are welcome.

We strive for excellence

Dr. T. Mupundu ( Principal)
