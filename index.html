Our Mission

To provide personalised holistic education that addresses learners' unique needs, cultivating independent thinking, that enables them to contribute to their communities in meaningful ways and succeed in the challenging world we live in.

Why Choose Us?

Smart Boards

We are always abreast with technological advancements. All our classes are equipped with interactive smartboards. These make learning easy, interactive, clean and enjoyable. These boards are also connected to the internet for easy and quick lookup of information and diagrams. The learning environment we provide is unparralled in Zimbabwe.

Solar Powered

Learning at Sandon is not interupted by power supplies. The whole institution from classes to hostels is powered by solar panels which supply electricity whenever there is disruption to the national power supply. Our students are able to perfom their experiments and partake in their studies without interruptions.

Excellent Menu

We employ fully qualified and experienced Chefs who prepare world class dishes for our students. We also offer a wide variety of dishes and options. This makes our menus excellent and world class. our students always enjoy those excellent meals in the comfort of our spacious dining hall.

Excellent Results

Sandon Academy has been consistantly producing top results for both A and O level Cambridge Syllabi. Our passrate is always around and near the 100% mark. We have also managed to produce some of the highest points at A level in the country. Our current record is at 29 Points in Science subjects.

Cambridge Syllabi

We offer Cambridge International Syllabi at both O and A Levels whose results are internationally recognised. Our students have no problems when applying to varius institutions of higher learning. New students coming from other schools can also easily fit in our ecosystem.

Highly Qualified Teachers

We employ highly qualified and experienced teachers. These are well trained, well paid, dedicated, hardworking and mature individuals who work around the clock to ensure the success of our students. These teachers have been producing excellent results for years.

1 : 20 Teacher pupil ratio

Our classes are small and mangeable. Each class has a maximum of 20 students. Teachers can efficiently deliver content to students and monitor their perfomance and progress and make informed decisions on how to help each individual student.

24/7 Internet

We have high speed fiber internet that is available 24 / 7 throughoutt the whole term. It helps students and teachers to conduct research at any given time. It also enables them to download useful supplementary study materials such as videos, journals and textbooks.
